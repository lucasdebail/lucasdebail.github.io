**About**

Air traffic controller, applied linguist, and educator with ten years of operational experience at Curitiba Approach Control and four years as an On-the-Job Training Instructor (OJTI). Taught and examined Aviation English under ICAO DOC 9835, ensuring operational Level 4 proficiency of personnel. Holds an M.A. in Applied Linguistics and is a Ph.D. student at Georgia State University. Has presented at multiple conferences and contributes to human factors work through the International Air Safety's (ISASI) Human Factors Working Group (HFWG) and the Language as a Human Factor in Aviation (LHUFT) committee.

Research Interests: Aviation English, ESP, Language Policy, Speech Intelligibility, Corpus Linguistics, Language Assessment, Curriculum Development

You may contact me at lribas1@gsu.edu
